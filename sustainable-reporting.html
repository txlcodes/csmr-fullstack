GRI Standards (Global Reporting Initiative)

The GRI Standards are the most widely used sustainability reporting framework globally. They provide a comprehensive set of standards for organizations to report on their economic, environmental, and social impacts.

Universal Standards

  • GRI 1: Foundation
  • GRI 2: General Disclosures
  • GRI 3: Material Topics

Topic Standards

  • Economic Topics (200 series)
  • Environmental Topics (300 series)
  • Social Topics (400 series)

Key Features:

  • Materiality assessment guidance
  • Stakeholder engagement requirements
  • Comprehensive disclosure requirements
  • Third-party assurance support
