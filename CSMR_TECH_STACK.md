# CSMR Tech Stack

## What is CSMR?
**CSMR (Centre for Sustainability & Management Research)** is an academic publishing platform that publishes cutting-edge research in sustainability, innovation, and responsible management practices. It provides peer-reviewed journals, certification programs, conferences, and research resources for scholars, researchers, and industry experts across India and beyond.
