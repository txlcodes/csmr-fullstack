Corporate Sustainability Reporting & Consulting

Empowering organisations to measure, manage, and enhance their sustainability performance with precision, transparency, and global compliance.

At CSMR, we partner with corporates to build robust sustainability systems aligned with global standards such as IFRS S1 & S2, BRSR, GRI, and SDGs. Our consulting services help organisations transition toward responsible, climate-conscious, and future-ready business practices.

Our Expertise

  • Sustainability Performance Tracking Comprehensive assessment of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) indicators to help organisations identify gaps, strengths, and improvement opportunities.
  • Carbon Accounting & Emission Analysis End-to-end support in measuring Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions, enabling organisations to build accurate carbon inventories and climate risk profiles.
  • Carbon Footprint Monitoring Continuous monitoring of carbon-intensive activities, energy use, resource efficiency, and waste patterns to support decarbonisation strategies.
  • Sustainability Reporting & Disclosure Preparation of high-quality sustainability reports aligned with mandatory and voluntary frameworks—covering ESG performance, climate risk, impact metrics, and future action plans.
  • Strategy & Implementation Support We assist corporates in developing sustainability roadmaps, net-zero strategies, materiality assessments, and capability-building programmes.
