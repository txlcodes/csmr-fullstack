Aims & Scope

Journal of Sustainability and Management Research is a multidisciplinary platform that publishes high-quality manuscripts at the intersection of sustainability and management.

The journal's focus includes key areas such as Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG), Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), carbon accounting, and the role of technology in advancing sustainability.

Mission

To advance knowledge and research that fosters sustainable practices and responsible management for a better future.

Scope

  • ESG performance measurement and reporting
  • The impact of CSR on corporate strategy and investor relations
  • The integration of SDGs into corporate governance
  • Innovations in carbon accounting and carbon reduction strategies
  • The application of advanced technologies for enhancing sustainability
  • Management practices that support sustainable development and environmental responsibility

It aims to engage scholars, practitioners, and policymakers by offering interdisciplinary insights that bridge theory and practice across sustainability and management fields.

Review Process

The Journal of Sustainability and Management Research follows a rigorous double-blind peer review process to ensure impartiality and high academic standards.

In this process, both the authors and reviewers remain anonymous to each other, safeguarding the objectivity of evaluations.

Manuscripts are assessed based on their originality, contribution to the field, methodological rigor, and alignment with the journal's focus on sustainability and management.

This review process ensures the publication of high-quality, impactful research while maintaining integrity and fairness throughout the evaluation process.

Vision

To be a leading academic platform for promoting sustainability research and influencing global sustainability practices.

Instructions for Authors

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts should be 6,000-8,000 words in length
  • Use double-spaced, 12-point Times New Roman font
  • Include an abstract of 150-250 words
  • Provide 4-6 keywords
  • Follow APA citation style

Submission Process

  • Submit through our online submission system
  • Ensure all authors have read and agreed to the submission terms
  • Include a cover letter explaining the manuscript's contribution
  • Provide contact information for all authors

Review Timeline

  • Initial review: 2-3 weeks
  • Peer review: 4-6 weeks
  • Revision period: 4-6 weeks
  • Final decision: 2-3 weeks
